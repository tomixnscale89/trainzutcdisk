Q. Where can I get Trainz?
 Trainz went on sale in stores throughout North America in February 2002, followed by Australia in April and Europe in May.  It is also available for purchase direct from the Auran Online Shop.

Q. What computer system will I need to run Trainz effectively?
Minimum System Requirements
Intel Pentium II 400mhz processor
128 megs RAM
Nvidia TNT2 16mb video card
Usable settings:
640*480 display resolution
32 bit color depth
adjusted details: no shadows, low draw distance, low draw detail, low train detail

Recommended System
Intel Pentium III 733mhz processor or equivalent
256 megs RAM
Nvidia GeForce2 32mb video card
usable settings:
800*600 display resolution
32 bit color depth
adjusted details: shadows enabled, medium draw distance, medium draw detail, medium train detail

Q. What does the first release of Trainz include?
The first release of Trainz includes My Collection, Trainz Surveyor and Trainz Driver.  Trainz also comes with 15 highly detailed locomotives (in 38 different liveries) from 12 countries, 3 ready to use regional layouts and more than 50 individual items of rolling stock.  In addition to all that, Trainz ships with hundreds of objects for creating your own masterpieces and provides access to a veritable treasure trove of free downloads.
