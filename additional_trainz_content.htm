Trainz has been designed to allow users to create, download and share as much custom content as possible.  We have incorporated the facility to import custom locomotives, rolling stock, flora and fauna, track, bridges, buildings, vehicles, people, tunnels and whatever else you can think of to make your railway come alive.

New objects are being made available for download every day along with custom layouts, textures and additional features.

To make it all as easy as possible, Trainz includes a gmax™ game pack that allows users to create all kinds of 3D models for use in Trainz.  gmax™ is a free 3D modeling program from Discreet (makers of 3D Studio Max) that can be downloaded and updated from the Discreet website.

The range of objects and items that can be imported into the Trainz environment is almost limitless and as the project evolves, we expect to see even greater depth and variety in Trainz content.  

In fact, we have already seen the development of trams, snow textures, log cabins, graders, buses, a cow-shaped locomotive, new bridges, retaining walls and countless other bits and pieces.  Just how useful these will be to the average Trainz fan remains to be seen but it’s a great illustration of just how customizable the Trainz environment can be.  

All of this great content can be downloaded from the Internet free of charge and even more will be available in official Trainz Collection Packs.
