Whilst Trainz offers you a lifetime of enjoyment on your PC at home, it has also fostered a unique community spirit. With a rapidly growing base of registered users, the Trainz online community includes more than 32,000* people from all over the world with a variety of backgrounds and interests.  It's a great way to meet people and get the latest information about the various aspects of Trainz, computer modeling or just about any other bits and pieces of information you might be looking for.

One of the great benefits to new Trainz users is the growing list of custom content created by Trainz fans for free download. Visit the Download Station and choose from the hundreds of objects made available.

You can also have 24 hour access to instant and experienced help via other Trainz community members on the online forum who are only too willing to help share their experience.

We're proud to have developed such a strong and friendly community around our product and we know that anybody with an interest in Trainz will feel welcome anytime.

We look forward to seeing you online. 

*as at August 2002.
