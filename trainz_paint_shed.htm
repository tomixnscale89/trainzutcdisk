Trainz Paint Shed provides a set of templates that help you design and create your very own railroad liveries. Follow the simple 5 step process and you will have a yard full of customised loco's and rolling stock before you know it.
  • Choose your own colour scheme
  • Incorporate your own railroad logos, motifs and slogans or select from the hundreds provided
  • Paint running numbers and reporting marks on locos and rolling stock
  • Open architecture design allows 3rd party developer expansion
  • Includes twenty new locomotives and fourteen new pieces of rolling stock

Note: Trainz The list of Paint Shed template stock is increasing all the time. Trainz V1.3 adds more than a dozen new locomotives to be repainted and new templates will be made available in future releases. Paint Shed's open architecture design also means you can create your own templates and share these with other users.
