Trainz is a complete railroad simulation that offers something for every train fan. Start by building stunningly realistic environments using the powerful, yet easy-to-use Surveyor tools. Next, select from dozens of loco's, passenger cars or freight wagons from around the world to assemble your trains, then jump into the cab to ride the rails. Choose your camera position, zoom in and out, switch between trains, couple and decouple, or take charge of a passenger run. You are limited only by your imagination.

Visit the Download Station to discover hundreds of new layouts, engines, wagons and scenery objects and add to your collection for free. You can even make your own scenery objects or trains using the Gmax 3D editor included on the disk.

With fans of all ages, from 7 to 77, and a rapidly growing global community at www.virtualtrainz.com, people can share their creations online, find tips and tricks, or simply discuss their favorite topics.

Trainz is a long-term project and is continually being upgraded and new features added. Already it provides a lifetime of enjoyment and there is still much more to come.
