The Trainz 'Driver’ module provides a rare opportunity to jump inside the cab of any of your collection of locomotives and cruise around the layout of your dreams.

You can also use a virtual DCC controller and take charge of as many trains as your PC power will allow.  

Switching between trains, decoupling and following signals is made easy by simple mouse click operation.  Variable weather, time compression and train physics options provide a customizable level of realism to satisfy all kinds of railroad pursuits from a drag racing derailment derby to the methodical progress of a heavy coal route across vast and desolate terrain.
