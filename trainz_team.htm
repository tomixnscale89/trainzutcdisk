Affectionately known as the 'Brew Crew', the Trainz development team consists of a dedicated nucleus of programmers and artists supported by an equally dedicated team of Beta Testers and Auran staff.

The 'Brew Crew' artists are unusual amongst game developers because they have a background in primarily architectural and technical design.  The nature of the Trainz project is such that we require significant technical skills and the artists were chosen specifically for their talents in these areas.
