The Trainz 'Surveyor’ module is where the you can really begin to create.  With a full set of trackside objects, buildings, flora and fauna, 'Surveyor' allows you to create a fully featured layout.  

The easy-to-use interface means even a complete novice can mold the landscape to reveal rolling hills, lakes, mountains, beaches, snow drifts or any other geographical formation they can imagine.  

You can watch as water ripples under bridges, sun shines through tunnels and cars and trucks go about their business on traffic laden roads.  Place people at stations, clotheslines in backyards and tractors on farms.  Ultimately, the uses for 'Surveyor' are endless and when placed in the hands of a creative person, imagination is the only limitation.
