We know that rail fans can never get enough content, and so we aim to provide a continuing selection of fantastic Trainz Add-Ons. This first of these Add-Ons, Trainz Paint Shed, was released in March 2002. 

Paint Shed is a paint tool that makes it easy to paint locomotives and rolling stock in your own unique color scheme. You can choose from pre-made templates, import your favourite railroad logos or make up your own. It also comes with 4 new loco's and 14 items of rolling stock to add to your collection

As the Trainz project progresses, Auran will provide Collection Packs featuring a selection of additional content including locomotives, rolling stock, trackside objects, layouts and a variety of third party content.  These Collection Packs will be available for order from the Trainz website through the Auran Online Shop.
